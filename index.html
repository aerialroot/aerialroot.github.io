Where Everything Starts

Well, this is where everything starts. Now I even don’t have a blog – I’m edting a markdown file on the VScode editor. Though on this second I don’t have a blog website yet, this is my first blog (formly).

What’s This Blog Website For

Later I will build more website, but what this website for? For my job, literally, for my job. I will put this website on my resume and it will have following features:

  1. This is a website about programming. I will put down everything about my programming experience here, including programming concept, my personal view about programming, how I code my projects on my resume, how I apply internships and jobs ect.
  2. Bilingual mode – English and French. This is because I will apply jobs both in Franch and North America. I won’t mix these two languages. First I will write a blog in English then I will write the same article in French. Though I’m not a native English speaker, my English is way better than my French. The content of whole website will be written in two language perspectively and can be changed by clicking the button.

What’s In This Website

  1. My perspective about porgramming. I will keep learning every field of programming and putting down my perspective of the concepts and techniques.
  2. Programming resorces. I will collect information during my learning progress and share them on my blog.
  3. The ideas and every step of my projects. When I’m coding my own project, I will record the whole progress, from the original ideas to how I acheieve every function goal.
  4. The techniques about blogs, including writting skills, layout, markdown and Latex etc.
  5. Something relaxing – manacode project. I will build a magic fantasy system based on programming knowledge. Every traditional magic will be designed like programming. And I will use these fantasy design in my novels and vedio games.
